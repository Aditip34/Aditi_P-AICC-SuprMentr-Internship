Text data often contains noise which makes it difficult to process.

1. Issues Identified
Slang words (e.g., “u” instead of “you”)
Emojis 
Typos and spelling mistakes
Mixed casing (UPPER/lower)
Unnecessary punctuation

2. Preprocessing Needed
Convert text to lowercase
Remove punctuation and emojis
Correct spelling errors
Remove stopwords
Normalize slang words

Clean text is essential for accurate NLP model performance.
